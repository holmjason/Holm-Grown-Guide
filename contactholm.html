Weather

I hope every day I guide is a beautiful day, but with that being said we know how mother nature is.

I will be keeping an eye on the weather before every trip to make the safest decision.

If I have to cancel due to weather there is a full refund and I apologize, but safety is my #1 concern!

You should dress according to the weather and always plan to bring extra clothes just in case.
