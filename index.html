Welcome To Holm Grown Guide Service

Introduction

I'm Jason! Welcome to my guide page and thank you for considering me for a fantastic fishing trip. This page is currently being worked on to make it more navigable and easier to use. Please stay tuned as the page develops. I am hoping for open water beginning of April and will start guiding as soon as the lakes have been declared "iced out". To start the year, I will be mostly concentrating on crappies and sunfish on Minnetonka. I'm excited to have you in the boat for a great time on the water and a fun filled fishing trip.

Expectations

The goal is to put you on the fish for fun relaxing day on the water with several nice catches.

I have all the gear from rods, reels, and tackle that you will need, prerigged!

I will meet you at the landing with the necessary , fishing and safety equipment and the boat, ready to go soon as you arrive!

Type of Fishing available

  • Crappies
  • Bass Fishing
  • Pike Fishing
  • Fall Walleyes

What You May Need To Bring

  • MUST HAVE current MN Fishing License!!! No exceptions. This will be verified before we leave the shore and anyone not in possession of their license will not be allowed to fish. No refunds.
  • Bug spray
  • Sunscreen
  • Beverages (non alcoholic please. Anyone showing obvious signs of unsafe impairment will either be denied boarding or returned to the shore without refund.)
  • Snacks
  • Jacket or rain gear (depending on weather)
